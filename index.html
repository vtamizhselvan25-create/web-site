Who We Are

About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation was founded on September 23, 2020 by Mr. Govind Shukla, with a powerful vision: to create lasting social impact across India. It is a Section 8 registered non-profit, licensed by the Central Government.

Headquartered in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, InAmigos actively operates across 28 states in India, addressing critical social issues through food distribution, education, women empowerment, animal welfare, and environmental sustainability.

Driven by a network of 200+ passionate volunteers, InAmigos believes in the power of collective action — every contribution, big or small, creates a ripple of positive change.

What We Do

Our 6 Core Projects

From hunger relief to environmental conservation — each project targets a critical need in India's most underserved communities.

🍱
Project SEVA
Food & Clothing Distribution

Providing essential meals and clothing to homeless, underprivileged, and elderly people. No one should sleep hungry or cold — that's our promise.

🍽️ 50,000+ meals & clothing distributed
📚
Project BACHPANSHALA
Education for Children

Bridging educational gaps by training underprivileged children in basic digital literacy, life skills, and school education support across India.

🎒 Thousands of children supported
🐾
Project JEEV
Animal Welfare

Rescuing, protecting, and feeding stray and injured animals. Every life matters — from street dogs to birds — we are their voice and care.

🐶 Animals rescued & fed daily
👩‍💼
Project UDAAN
Women Empowerment

Empowering women through skill development, financial independence training, and entrepreneurship programs to help them soar to new heights.

✊ Women gaining financial independence
🌿
Project PRAKRITI
Environmental Conservation

Driving tree plantation drives, cleanliness campaigns, and sustainability awareness to protect India's environment for future generations.

🌳 Trees planted across India
💼
Project VIKAS
Skill & Career Development

Enhancing employability of youth through internship programs, digital skills, and professional development training across all 28 states.
